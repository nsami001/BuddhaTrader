# 🌱 BuddhaTrader

> **From "Day One" to "One Day."**

BuddhaTrader is an open learning and research project for algorithmic trading.

Its purpose is **not** to promise profits. Its purpose is to help people learn, build, test, and improve trading strategies through evidence, discipline, and continuous learning.

---

# 🎯 Mission

To create a transparent, educational, and research-oriented trading platform where every strategy is:

- Understood before it is coded.
- Tested before it is trusted.
- Improved using data instead of opinions.
- Shared to help others learn.

# ⚠️ Before You Trade

Professional pilots use flight simulators.

Professional drivers practice before racing.

Professional traders paper trade before risking money.

BuddhaTrader follows the same philosophy.

Before using any strategy with real money:

- Learn how it works.
- Backtest it.
- Paper trade it.
- Understand its risks.
- Only then consider live trading.

# ❌ What BuddhaTrader Is NOT

BuddhaTrader does **not** promise:

- "Make money quickly."
- "95% profitable."
- "Guaranteed returns."

Markets are uncertain.

Every strategy has periods of success and periods of failure.

Our goal is not to eliminate uncertainty.

Our goal is to understand it, measure it, and manage it responsibly.
